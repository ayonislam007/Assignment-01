Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about DevConf 2026.

What is DevConf 2026?

DevConf 2026 is a developer conference featuring expert speakers, technical sessions, workshops, and networking opportunities.

The conference will take place at the International Convention City Bashundhara (ICCB), Dhaka.

What does my ticket include?

Your ticket includes access to all sessions, keynote speeches, workshops, networking events, and refreshments.

Yes. Refund requests are accepted up to 14 days before the event.

Yes. Complimentary lunch, coffee, and snacks will be available throughout the conference.
